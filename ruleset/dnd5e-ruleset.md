# D&D 5e Ruleset Reference (2024 Edition)

## Quick Combat Rules

### Actions in Combat
- **Action**: Cast a spell, Attack, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Use Object
- **Bonus Action**: Secondary action available most turns (varies by class/spell)
- **Reaction**: Triggered by specific events (opportunity attack, shield spell, etc.)
- **Free Interaction**: Draw/stow a weapon, open a door, etc. (one per turn)

### Ability Checks & Saves
- **DC (Difficulty Class)**: 10 (easy), 15 (moderate), 20 (hard), 25+ (near impossible)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +2 (levels 1-4), +3 (5-8), +4 (9-12), +5 (13-16), +6 (17-20)
- **Advantage/Disadvantage**: Roll twice, take higher/lower

### Combat Flow
1. **Initiative**: d20 + DEX modifier
2. **Turns**: Each creature takes one turn in order
3. **End of Turn**: Movement, action, bonus action, reaction, free interaction
4. **Concentration**: Can maintain one spell requiring concentration at a time

### Damage & Healing
- **Damage**: Roll stated dice + modifiers
- **Healing**: Restore HP (max HP is cap, no overheal)
- **Death Saves**: At 0 HP, make CON saves (DC 10) on failed turn
  - 3 failures = death
  - 3 successes = stable (0 HP but unconscious)
  - Natural 20 = regain 1 HP
  - Natural 1 = two failures

### Conditions (2024 Edition)
- **Blinded**: Can't see, -4 to hit, auto-miss on attacks
- **Charmed**: Can't harm the charmer, advantage on WIS saves against charmer
- **Deafened**: Can't hear
- **Frightened**: Disadvantage on checks/saves while can see source, can't willingly move toward source
- **Grappled**: Speed 0, can't benefit from increases to speed
- **Incapacitated**: Can't act or react
- **Invisible**: Unseen, advantage on attacks, disadvantage against attacks on you
- **Paralyzed**: Incapacitated, automatically fail STR/DEX saves, attacks have advantage
- **Petrified**: Incapacitated, treated as nonmagical object, resistances/immunities don't apply
- **Poisoned**: Disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks
- **Prone**: Can't move, disadvantage on melee attack, melee attacks within 5ft have advantage
- **Restrained**: Speed 0, disadvantage on DEX saves/attacks
- **Stunned**: Incapacitated, automatically fail STR/DEX saves, attacks have advantage
- **Unconscious**: Incapacitated, can't move/speak, unaware of surroundings, hits auto-crit if within 5ft

### Common Save DCs
- **Spell Save DC**: 8 + spell level + ability modifier
- **Ability DC**: 10 + proficiency + relevant ability modifier

### Melee Attacks
- **Reach**: 5 feet (normal), 10+ feet (reach weapon/ability)
- **Opportunity Attack**: Reaction when enemy leaves reach (no if incapacitated/surprised)

### Spellcasting
- **Spell Slots**: Limited per level, replenish on long rest
- **Cantrips**: At-will spells, no slots used
- **Concentration**: One active concentration spell at a time
- **Components**: Verbal (can't cast silenced), Somatic (need hand free), Material (specified cost)

### Resting
- **Short Rest**: 1 hour, regain some HP/resources
- **Long Rest**: 8 hours, regain all HP, slots, some class features

## 2024 Edition Major Changes (PHB 2024)

### Ability Score Improvements (ASI) - NEW TIMING
- **Level 4, 8, 12, 16, 19**: +2 to ability score OR +1 to two scores OR gain a feat
- *Old (2014)*: Levels 4, 8, 12, 16, 19, 20
- *Change*: Simplified progression, more consistent feat access

### Feat System Overhaul
- **New feat structure**: Each feat now has prerequisites/requirements
- **Feat bonuses**: Many feats grant +1 ability score (combat more powerful)
- **Key new feats**: Alert, Tough, Magic Initiate (revised), Polearm Master (revised), War Caster
- **Notable changes**:
  - Great Weapon Master: Requires Strength 13+
  - Crossbow Expert: Requires Dexterity 13+
  - Sharpshooter: Requires Dexterity 13+

### Background System Redesign
- **Ability Score Bonuses**: Each background grants +2/+1 ability score increases
- **Feat at 1st level**: Can choose a feat instead of standard ability increase
- **Background benefits**: Equipment, skill proficiencies, languages tied to background choice
- **Examples**:
  - Folk Hero: +2 Wisdom, +1 Strength
  - Acolyte: +2 Wisdom, +1 Intelligence
  - Soldier: +2 Strength, +1 Constitution

### Class-Specific Changes (2024 PHB)

#### Barbarian
- **Primal Path (subclass)**: Choose at 3rd level (not 5th)
- **Reckless Attack**: Now explicitly triggers if Barbarian is reckless or target attacks recklessly
- **Unarmored Defense**: Now adds CON modifier to AC (same as before, clarified)
- **Rage**: Bonus to melee damage scaling improved

#### Bard
- **Spellcasting Focus**: Can use instrument as focus
- **Bardic Inspiration**: Now uses d8 base (scaling: d8, d10, d12 at certain levels)
- **Jack of All Trades**: Bonus increases at higher levels

#### Cleric
- **Channel Divinity**: Uses Proficiency Bonus for save DC
- **Spellcasting**: Gains additional spell slots at key levels
- **Divine Domain**: Subclass features now more impactful at lower levels

#### Druid
- **Circle of the Moon**: Wildshape now scales with class levels
- **Wildshape forms**: Can be used more frequently (resource management improved)
- **Spellcasting**: Preparation remains Intelligence-based

#### Fighter
- **Extra Attack**: Granted at 5th level (unchanged from 2014)
- **Fighting Styles**: New options available (Blessed Warrior, Interception, Superior Technique)
- **Ability Score Improvements**: Still at 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14, 16, 19

#### Monk
- **SIGNIFICANT REDESIGN**:
  - **Martial Arts**: Now uses d8 damage base (d10 at 17th)
  - **Ki Points**: Changed to use Wisdom modifier differently
  - **Monastic Tradition**: Choose at 3rd level
  - **Stunning Strike**: Now limited to once per creature per turn
  - **Unarmored Movement**: Gains more movement options

#### Paladin
- **Smite spells**: Smite now castable as bonus action
- **Divine Smite**: Damage scaling adjusted
- **Channel Divinity**: More options per rest

#### Ranger
- **MAJOR OVERHAUL**:
  - **Spellcasting**: Now gained at 1st level (previously 5th)
  - **Ranger's Quarry**: New mechanic (bonus damage to tracked enemy)
  - **Drakewarden subclass**: New subclass option
  - **Favored Enemy/Terrain**: Simplified mechanics
  - **Spells Known**: Increases with level progression

#### Rogue
- **Expertise**: Can add proficiency bonus twice to d20 rolls
- **Sneak Attack**: Now triggers more consistently
- **Cunning Action**: Established baseline, subclass variations exist
- **Arcane Trickster**: Spell list updated

#### Sorcerer
- **Metamagic**: Reworked mechanics for several options
- **Sorcerous Versatility**: Can swap spells/metamagics on rest
- **Draconic Bloodline**: Updated with new features
- **Font of Magic**: Scaling adjusted

#### Warlock
- **Invocations**: New options, some redesigned
- **Pact Boon**: Subclass choice now clearer
- **Spell Slot Recovery**: Still short-rest based (key difference from other casters)
- **Eldritch Blast**: Remains baseline, Invocations enhance

#### Wizard
- **Spell Preparation**: Based on Intelligence modifier + Wizard level
- **Spellbook**: Still required, can copy spells during rests
- **Wizard Schools**: Subclass benefits appear earlier
- **Cantrip scaling**: Added at higher levels

### Combat Changes (2024 Edition)

#### Initiative
- *Unchanged*: Still d20 + DEX modifier
- *Clarification*: Ties handled by higher DEX, then DM discretion

#### Multiattack (Monsters)
- Monster attacks now clearer on action economy
- "Multiattack" action specifies attack sequence (e.g., "2 claws + 1 bite")

#### Opportunity Attacks
- Still trigger when foe leaves melee range
- Still no attack if incapacitated, unaware, or has taken Disengage action

#### Action Surge (Fighter)
- Bonus action attack (previously "one weapon attack")
- Now explicitly "one melee weapon attack"

### Spellcasting Updates (2024 Edition)

#### Cantrip Scaling (by Class)
- **Bard**: d6 (1st), d8 (5th), d10 (11th), d12 (17th)
- **Cleric**: d6 (1st), d8 (5th), d10 (11th), d12 (17th)
- **Druid**: d6 (1st), d8 (5th), d10 (11th), d12 (17th)
- **Sorcerer**: d6 (1st), d8 (5th), d10 (11th), d12 (17th)
- **Wizard**: d6 (1st), d8 (5th), d10 (11th), d12 (17th)
- **Warlock**: d6 (1st), d8 (6th), d10 (11th), d12 (17th)

#### Spell Slots (Full Casters: Cleric, Druid, Wizard)
- **Bard**: Half-caster progression (up to 4th level spells)
- **Sorcerer**: Full-caster progression
- **Warlock**: Short-rest recovery (unique mechanic)

#### Key Spell Changes
- **Cure Wounds**: Removed from spell list (no longer valid)
- **Magic Missile**: Now 1d4+1 per projectile (was 1d4+1 total)
- **Revivify**: Requires 300gp diamond (clarified)
- **Concentration**: Rules unchanged (one active spell per creature)

#### Concentration Spells - Key Examples
- **Bless**: +1d4 to attacks/saves, 1 minute concentration
- **Fly**: 60ft flight, 10 minutes concentration
- **Greater Invisibility**: Invisible but can attack, 1 minute concentration
- **Hold Person**: Restrains target, 1 minute concentration (save each turn)

### Equipment & Item Changes (2024 Edition)

#### Weapon Mastery (NEW)
- **Added mechanic**: Some martial weapons gain bonus effects at higher levels
- **Examples**: Longsword gains bonus on parry, Greataxe gains extra damage
- **Availability**: Limited to certain weapon types

#### Armor Changes
- Base AC values **unchanged** from 2014
- **Light**: 11 + DEX (leather, studded leather)
- **Medium**: 12-14 + DEX (half plate, scale mail, 14 AC half-plate requires disadvantage on stealth)
- **Heavy**: 15-18 AC, no DEX (plate mail = 18 AC, no DEX)

#### Shield
- **+2 AC** (unchanged)
- **Interaction**: Now explicitly uses object interaction (draw/stow shield as free action)

### Proficiency & Ability Checks (2024 Edition)

#### Proficiency Bonus Progression
- Same as 2014 edition: +2 (1-4), +3 (5-8), +4 (9-12), +5 (13-16), +6 (17-20)

#### Skill Proficiencies
- **Acrobatics** (DEX), **Animal Handling** (WIS), **Arcana** (INT)
- **Athletics** (STR), **Deception** (CHA), **History** (INT)
- **Insight** (WIS), **Intimidation** (CHA), **Investigation** (INT)
- **Medicine** (WIS), **Nature** (INT), **Perception** (WIS)
- **Performance** (CHA), **Persuasion** (CHA), **Religion** (INT)
- **Sleight of Hand** (DEX), **Stealth** (DEX), **Survival** (WIS)

#### Expertise (Rogue/Bard)
- **Rogue**: 2 skills at 1st, +1 every 4 levels (8 total at 20th)
- **Bard**: Same progression as Rogue
- **Effect**: Add proficiency bonus twice to d20 roll (not passive skill checks)

### Multiclassing Rules (2024 Edition)

#### Prerequisites
- Need 13+ in relevant ability score for each class
- Example: Monk requires 13 DEX + WIS, Wizard requires 13 INT

#### Spell Slot Progression
- Combine full-caster levels (Cleric, Druid, Wizard, Sorcerer)
- Half-casters (Bard, Ranger, Warlock uses own rules)
- Warlocks get separate pact slot progression (short-rest)

#### Feat at 1st Level
- Can now take a feat instead of +2/+1 ability scores at character creation
- Must meet feat prerequisites

### Death & Resurrection (2024 Edition)

#### Death Saves (Unchanged)
- At 0 HP, make CON save DC 10 at start of turn
- 3 successes = stable (unconscious but safe)
- 3 failures = dead
- Natural 20 = regain 1 HP and consciousness
- Natural 1 = two failures
- Taking damage while unconscious = instant failure

#### Resurrection
- **Revivify** (3rd level): 1 minute window, 300gp diamond
- **Raise Dead** (5th level): Up to 10 days dead, 500gp diamond
- **Resurrection** (7th level): Up to 100 years dead, 1000gp diamond
- **True Resurrection** (9th level): No time limit, 25000gp diamond
- **Clone** (8th level): Insurance against permanent death

### Variant Rules & Options (2024 PHB)

#### Flanking (Optional)
- If enabled: +2 to melee attack rolls when ally within 5ft of target
- *Note*: Not enabled in standard rules, DM decision

#### Healing Surges (Removed)
- No longer part of 2024 PHB (was variant in 2014)
- Hit Dice remain (short rest recovery)

#### Hit Dice Recovery
- **Short Rest**: Spend 1+ Hit Dice, regain HP = roll + CON modifier
- **Long Rest**: Regain all Hit Dice (max = character level)

## Summary of Most Impactful 2024 Changes

1. **ASI at levels 4, 8, 12, 16, 19** — More consistent feat access
2. **Background ability bonuses** — Stronger character creation customization
3. **Ranger spellcasting at 1st level** — Major buff to Ranger viability
4. **Monk redesign (d8 base damage, Ki changes)** — Significant mechanical shift
5. **Feat overhaul with prerequisites** — More balanced feat selection
6. **Bard/Rogue d8 hit die expectation** — Clearer survivability math
